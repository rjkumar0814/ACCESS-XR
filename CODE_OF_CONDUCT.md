# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of ACCESS-XR, pledge to make participation in our project and community a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to fostering an open, inclusive, and collaborative environment that supports responsible research and scientific integrity.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Treating everyone with respect and professionalism.
- Being constructive when providing feedback.
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully.
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences.
- Focusing discussions on technical and scientific issues.
- Supporting reproducible and ethical research.
- Acknowledging the work and contributions of others.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or insulting comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberate intimidation or trolling.
- Plagiarism or misrepresentation of research.
- Sharing malicious code or intentionally disrupting the project.

---

## Research Integrity

Contributors are expected to:

- Report experimental results honestly.
- Clearly distinguish reproduced results from original contributions.
- Cite external datasets, software, and publications appropriately.
- Respect dataset licenses and copyright restrictions.
- Follow accepted ethical practices in AI and accessibility research.

---

## Responsibilities of Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing contributions fairly and objectively.
- Maintaining respectful discussions.
- Providing constructive technical feedback.
- Enforcing this Code of Conduct consistently.
- Protecting contributors from harassment or abusive behavior.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub Issues
- Pull Requests
- Discussions
- Project documentation
- Source code contributions
- Project-related communications

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take appropriate action, including:

- Requesting changes to behavior.
- Removing inappropriate comments.
- Rejecting contributions.
- Temporarily restricting participation.
- Permanently banning contributors for repeated or serious violations.
